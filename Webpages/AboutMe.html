About James Goring

James Goring was an average person who worked for his wage and did various jobs in his life. One of those jobs was working away in a bakery, a job that he particularly enjoyed. Early mornings and an early finish time, what's not to love?

However, James couldn't sustain himself on just minimum wage, actually he is surprised that anyone can sustain themselves on minimum wage. The government should really force the hand of larger employers to pay a decent wage, but he doesn't need to say this as the pandemic really left corporations short-changed with workers. They say that karma is circular.

Anyways, James Goring is a mature student (i.e. in his 30s) and giving it his best shot to become something more than just minimum wage. More importantly, it is something he is geniunely interested in and that course is Computer Sciences.

For one of his modules, he has been tasked with creating the most exciting and interesting website and what more exiciting than baked goods? Obviously Computer Sciences but he couldn't possibly bang on about that. So instead he is doing baked goods, luckily something he has experience in.
